# 📊 Sales Dashboard - Power BI Project

## Overview
This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across multiple dimensions such as time, geography, and product categories.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify top-performing customers and regions  
- Monitor monthly profit trends  
- Evaluate returns across product categories  
- Support data-driven decision making  

---

##  Key Features
- **Sales & KPIs:**  
  - Total Sales: 103M  
  - Total Profit: 23M  
  - Total Cost: 80M  
  - Quantity Sold: 199K  

- **Channel Analysis:**  
  Comparison between Wholesale and Retail performance  

- **Time Analysis:**  
  Monthly profit trend to identify patterns and seasonality  

- **Geographical Analysis:**  
  Interactive map showing quantity distribution by country  

- **Customer Insights:**  
  Top customers ranked by profit contribution  

- **Returns Analysis:**  
  Breakdown of returns by item category  

---

## Filters & Interactivity
The dashboard includes dynamic filters for:
- Item Name  
- Year  
- Country  

All visuals are interconnected to enable deep data exploration.

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Data Modeling  
- DAX  


## Key Insights
- Wholesale channel generates the highest share of sales  
- Profit shows fluctuations across months indicating seasonal trends  
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total profit  
- Certain product categories have higher return rates  



## Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders to:
- Improve sales strategies  
- Identify high-value customers and markets  
- Reduce returns and increase profitability  
- Make faster and more accurate decisions
